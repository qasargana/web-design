ABOUT

MERIDIAN CLINICAL TRIALS

Meridian Clinical Trials (MCT) serves as a semiannual symposium
orchestrated by Continuum Globe Ltd, serving as an invaluable resource for leaders within the Clinical Trials industry. The event fosters the exchange of knowledge, facilitates networking opportunities, enables crucial meetings, and promotes partner outreach.

Our collaborative efforts involve partnering with prominent
pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies, Contract Research
Organizations (CROs), Research Sites, and affiliated organizations,
with the aim of providing our audience with cutting-edge insights and
solutions. The 9th MCT’s comprehensive agenda is designed to assist
companies in enhancing their study design and overall strategy
pertaining to outsourcing, clinical operations, and development. Our
ultimate objective is to aid our partners in expediting their pipelines by
granting them access to the most up-to-date knowledge, a platform
for meetings, and facilitating the right connections for acquiring
funding. Within a scientific environment, MCT serves as an incubator
for companies to engage in discussions regarding the prevailing
business trends.
